Our Story

Welcome To Labor Cafe

Cookie cutter coffee shops kill creativity. Enamored by the cozy vibey cafes of Europe and disenchanted with current coworking scene, we bring you the first vegan coworking coffee shop in the US. Sit back, pull out your laptop and get work done surrounded by other creators.

Take advantage of our coworking facilities, faultless wifi, and plentiful outlets. Connect and exchange skills with like minded freelancers, record your podcast, hold a meeting in a relaxed yet inspirational space, join our weekly networking breakfast. No membership, no contracts, no hustle. We're just your new favorite coffee shop. With benefits.

Fuel your body with non-pretentious LA-roasted coffee, invigorating smoothies and plant based wholesome snacks. Fuel your mind with our meditation workshops and thought provoking movie nights. We exist to help get your creative juices flowing and we take a holistic approach. Mens sana in corpore sano.
